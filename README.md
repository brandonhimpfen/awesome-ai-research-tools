## Experimentation & Notebooks

- [JupyterLab](https://github.com/jupyterlab/jupyterlab) – The de-facto environment for interactive research notebooks.
- [Google Colab](https://colab.research.google.com/) – Free GPU-enabled notebooks for research and prototyping.
- [Kaggle Notebooks](https://www.kaggle.com/code) – Notebook environment with free GPUs and large datasets.
- [Deepnote](https://deepnote.com/) – Collaborative research notebooks with real-time editing.

## Model Training & Experiments

- [PyTorch](https://github.com/pytorch/pytorch) – Most widely used deep learning framework in research.
- [TensorFlow](https://github.com/tensorflow/tensorflow) – Full-stack ML framework from Google.
- [JAX](https://github.com/google/jax) – High-performance, functional ML framework used in cutting-edge research.
- [HuggingFace Transformers](https://github.com/huggingface/transformers) – Library of pretrained models and research-ready architectures.
- [Lightning](https://github.com/Lightning-AI/pytorch-lightning) – High-level framework for scalable model training.
- [Fast.ai](https://github.com/fastai/fastai) – Research-friendly training library built on PyTorch.
- [DeepSpeed](https://github.com/microsoft/DeepSpeed) – Training optimizations for large models.
- [Colossal-AI](https://github.com/hpcaitech/ColossalAI) – Distributed training and optimization for LLMs.

## Evaluation & Benchmarking

- [OpenAI Evals](https://github.com/openai/evals) – Framework for evaluating generative models.
- [HELM](https://crfm.stanford.edu/helm/latest/) – Holistic evaluation across diverse reasoning and safety metrics.
- [LM Evaluation Harness](https://github.com/EleutherAI/lm-evaluation-harness) – Standardized benchmark suite for LLMs.
- [RAGAS](https://github.com/explodinggradients/ragas) – Evaluation metrics for RAG systems.
- [Arize Phoenix](https://github.com/Arize-ai/phoenix) – Open-source observability and evaluation toolkit.
- [W&B Evaluation Tools](https://wandb.ai) – Model evaluation tracking and visualization.

## Visualization & Interpretability

- [TensorBoard](https://github.com/tensorflow/tensorboard) – Visualization suite for model training and metrics.
- [Weights & Biases](https://github.com/wandb/wandb) – Experiment dashboarding and visual analytics.
- [Captum](https://github.com/pytorch/captum) – Model interpretability for PyTorch.
- [LIT (Language Interpretability Tool)](https://github.com/PAIR-code/lit) – Interactive model analysis from Google PAIR.
- [Netron](https://github.com/lutzroeder/netron) – Neural network model visualizer (ONNX, PyTorch, TensorFlow).
- [Aletheia](https://github.com/obioberoi/aletheia) – Tools for AI interpretability and artifact inspection.

## Literature Search & Paper Tools

- [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/) – Primary repository for ML and AI research papers.
- [Papers With Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) – SOTA benchmarks and paper-to-code mappings.
- [ar5iv](https://ar5iv.labs.arxiv.org/) – Effortless HTML versions of arXiv papers.
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) – AI-powered academic search engine.
- [Connected Papers](https://www.connectedpapers.com/) – Visual graph exploration of related research.
- [ResearchRabbit](https://www.researchrabbit.ai/) – Visual discovery tool for academic literature networks.
- [Scholarcy](https://www.scholarcy.com/) – AI assistant for summarizing research papers.
- [ExplainPaper](https://www.explainpaper.com/) – Highlight sections of papers and get explanations.

## Dataset Tools

- [HuggingFace Datasets](https://github.com/huggingface/datasets) – Huge collection of ready-to-use datasets.
- [Kaggle Datasets](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets) – Public datasets across all domains.
- [TensorFlow Datasets](https://github.com/tensorflow/datasets) – Standardized datasets for ML research.
- [Dataset Search (Google)](https://datasetsearch.research.google.com/) – Search engine for public datasets.
- [NVIDIA NGC Datasets](https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/) – Vision, speech, and scientific datasets.
- [OpenML](https://www.openml.org/) – Platform for dataset exploration and benchmarking.

## Reproducibility & Experiment Tracking

- [Weights & Biases](https://github.com/wandb/wandb) – End-to-end experiment tracking and reproducibility tools.
- [MLflow](https://github.com/mlflow/mlflow) – Open-source experiment tracking, models, and pipelines.
- [DVC (Data Version Control)](https://github.com/iterative/dvc) – Git-style versioning for datasets and models.
- [Guild AI](https://github.com/guildai/guildai) – Experiment management for machine learning research.
- [Aim](https://github.com/aimhubio/aim) – Fast, open-source experiment tracker.
- [Reproducibility Checklist](https://paperswithcode.com/reproducibility) – Community guidelines for reproducible ML.

## Learning Resources

- [Deep Learning Book (Goodfellow)](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/) – The foundational text for modern deep learning.
- [Dive into Deep Learning](https://d2l.ai/) – Interactive, code-driven deep learning book.
- [Fast.ai Courses](https://course.fast.ai/) – Application-focused deep learning education.
- [Stanford CS231n](http://cs231n.stanford.edu/) – The classic computer vision and deep learning course.
- [MIT Intro to Deep Learning](https://introtodeeplearning.com/) – University course with hands-on labs.
- [HuggingFace Course](https://huggingface.co/learn) – Full curriculum for LLMs, transformers, and pipelines.
